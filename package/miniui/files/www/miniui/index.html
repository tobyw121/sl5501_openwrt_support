Firmware upgrade

Upload firmware to /tmp/ via SCP or provide an HTTPS URL. The router will reboot after a successful upgrade.

MiniUI uses rpcd ACLs for least-privilege access. Adjust settings with make menuconfig to include or exclude the interface.
